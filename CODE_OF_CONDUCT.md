# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone.

## Expectations
- Be respectful and considerate.
- Assume good intent and collaborate in good faith.
- Keep discussions constructive and on-topic.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or hostile behavior.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers.
